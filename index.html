    Football, also known as association football or soccer, is a captivating game played by two teams, each consisting of 11 players. The objective is to maneuver the ball into the opposing team’s goal without using hands or arms. Only the goalkeeper is allowed to handle the ball, and even then, only within the penalty area surrounding the goal. The team that scores more goals emerges victorious. Here are some key points about football: Popularity: Football is the world’s most popular ball game, both in terms of participants and spectators. It can be played almost anywhere, from official football fields to gymnasiums, streets, school playgrounds, parks, and beaches. Origins: Modern football originated in Britain during the 19th century. It has since spread across the globe, captivating hearts and minds in every corner. Numbers: At the turn of the 21st century, there were approximately 250 million football players worldwide, with over 1.3 billion people interested in the sport. The quadrennial World Cup finals alone attract a combined television audience of more than 26 billion viewers.
